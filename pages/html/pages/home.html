Freshworks

Freshworks Inc. is a cloud-based software-as-a-service company, founded in 2010 in Chennai, India The company provides cloud-based tools for customer relationship management (CRM), IT service management (ITSM), and e-commerce marketing. As of October 2023, Freshworks has over 60,000 business clients. Among these, more than 51,000 are small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs), defined as companies with no more than 250 employees. On September 22, 2021, Freshworks became the first Indian SaaS company to be listed on Nasdaq.In its initial public offering, Freshworks raised $1.03 billion following earlier funding rounds from venture capital firms such as Accel and Google's venture capital fund, CapitalG.
